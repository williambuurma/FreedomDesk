Your front desk is doing two jobs at once

The phone rings while someone is checking in. A new patient calls during the morning rush. After hours, someone with a toothache gets voicemail instead of a real conversation.

It is not a staffing failure. It is what happens when one team is responsible for everyone walking through the door and everyone calling in.

FreedomDesk gives your office calm backup — fewer interruptions for your team, and patients who still feel looked after.

How it works

We set this up with your office manager or front desk lead — around how you already run things.

  1. 1

    Same phone number

    Patients call your office as they always have. When your team is busy or the office is closed, calls are still answered.

  2. 2

    Your rules, your workflow

    We learn your scheduling preferences, common questions, after-hours approach, and who to notify when something is urgent.

  3. 3

    Your team stays in the loop

    Call summaries go to the right people. Your staff follows up and keeps the patient relationship — nothing changes about who owns the care.
