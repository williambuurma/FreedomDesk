# Michigan Regional Notes — New Patient

West Michigan GP practices — classify insurance to **program level** (never treat "Delta" as one plan).

## Disambiguation priority

| Priority | Program | Caller cues |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Delta Dental **PPO** (incl. Premier) | Employer plan, "Delta through work" |
| 2 | Delta Dental **Medicaid** | Adult Medicaid card shows Delta |
| 3 | **Healthy Kids Dental (HKD)** | Child on state dental, "Healthy Kids" |
| 4 | **Michigan Medicaid** (adult) | "Medicaid," "MI Health," adult patient |
| 5 | Other **PPO** | BCBSM, Cigna, MetLife, etc. |
| 6 | Cash-pay / none | No insurance |

**When caller says "Delta" or "Delta Dental":**
> "Is that Delta Dental through your employer, or state insurance like Medicaid or Healthy Kids Dental?"

**Child on state/Medicaid dental → HKD**, not Delta PPO.

## New patient intake
- Collect carrier, program type, member ID or Medicaid ID if available
- Schedule Medicaid/HKD only if practice config lists the program as accepted
- Defer eligibility and benefits to front desk verification
